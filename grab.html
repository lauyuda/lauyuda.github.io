Grab App Proposal

Personification of the app user,
customer journey mapping to identify pain points,
and brainstorming ideas to come to a prototype.

New Proposed Features and Services

Own Personal Profile

The profile page will be used for others to view your profile. You can view names, age, profession, skills, and ongoing courses etc. This allows for people to understand your interests and strengths.

Freelance Page

This includes other possible freelancing opportunities with skills that you may picked up (possibly through hobbies). It provides a possible additional income source and users can filter results based on their skills and dates. Options available to save specific posts to reconfirm your own schedule before taking on the job.

Upskilling Courses

Provides a list of courses as offered by other institutes. Users can click to understand more about what the course entails. Includes information such as availibility of certain subsidies.

Career Coaching

Informs and direct you to third party organisations which will provide career support and coaching to help mid-careers make a transition.

Start Up

Connects you with a network of app users. You can pitch a start up, list your relevant skills, and look for like-minded people with complementary skillsets to fill the missing gaps. Or you can look at other ideas and find something which you are passionate about and with people you can work with.
